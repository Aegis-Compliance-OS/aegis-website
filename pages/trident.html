What Is TRIDENT

A sensing platform engineered for environments where physical precision and regulatory proof must coexist.

TRIDENT is a hardware and mixed-reality platform that captures the physical state of manufacturing, maintenance, and operational environments with quantifiable precision. It combines integrated sensor arrays with mixed-reality overlay technology to create authoritative digital twin records of physical processes as they happen.

The platform commercializes two U.S. Navy patents developed at the Naval Postgraduate School's MOVES Institute — covering both the authoring of digital twin environments and quantitative quality assurance scoring for mixed reality systems. These patents form the technical foundation for TRIDENT's ability to produce compliance-grade evidence from physical events.

STARS — the Secure Translational Automated Regulatory System — is embedded directly in TRIDENT, converting sensor output into framework-specific documentation without a separate integration step.

Core Capabilities

What TRIDENT does that no documentation workflow can replicate.

01

Markerless Digital Twin Authoring

Captures physical assets and environments into structured digital twin representations without physical markers or instrumentation of the target object. Enables documentation of as-built conditions for existing equipment and legacy systems.

02

Quantitative QA Scoring

Produces a quantifiable fidelity score for each digital twin capture against physical ground truth — not an estimate, not a visual inspection. This score is the technical basis for TRIDENT's admissibility as compliance evidence rather than documentation.

03

Mixed Reality Process Overlay

Overlays structured work instructions and process data directly onto the physical workspace during operations. Simultaneously captures technician actions and environmental conditions as time-stamped, georeferenced records for audit trail generation.

04

Real-Time Telemetry Capture

Collects environmental, spatial, and process telemetry continuously during operations. All sensor data is time-stamped, structured, and passed to STARS for regulatory translation — creating an unbroken chain of custody from physical event to compliance record.

05

Catalog Ingestion Engine

Ingests machine-readable regulatory standards directly into STARS. Adding a new framework — a new CMMC version, an emerging USCG cyber rulemaking, an industry-specific standard — requires updating the catalog, not re-architecting the platform.

06

Assessor-Ready Output

Documentation packages are structured at the point of generation for specific assessor and auditor requirements — no reformatting, no gap analysis, no post-hoc reconstruction. Evidence arrives organized to the exact clause, control, or requirement being assessed.

Embedded Intelligence

STARS is built into TRIDENT — not connected to it.

The Secure Translational Automated Regulatory System translates TRIDENT's sensor output into framework-specific regulatory language automatically. There is no integration step, no middleware, no manual translation layer between what TRIDENT captures and what assessors receive.
