Founder & Chief Executive Officer

Dana Mincey-Breaux

PMP · U.S. Army (Veteran) · TS/SCI

Dana brings 17 years of combined U.S. Army Signal Corps and DoD program management experience to Aegis. As a Signal Corps officer, she operated at the intersection of communications infrastructure, cybersecurity, and mission-critical systems — the same intersection where TRIDENT and STARS now sit.

Before founding Aegis, Dana managed complex DoD acquisition programs requiring simultaneous compliance across CMMC, ITAR, and DFARS frameworks. That firsthand experience with the compliance gap — the distance between what physical systems produce and what regulators require — is the founding problem Aegis exists to solve.

Dana holds an active Top Secret / Sensitive Compartmented Information clearance and is the named inventor on Provisional Patent 63/987,480, the STARS regulatory translation layer and cryptographic enforcement system. She is also the primary applicant on the DEVCOM CBC patent license for AURUM's MXene sensing substrate.
